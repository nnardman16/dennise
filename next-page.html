My Cutie Puny,

Happy 2nd Monthsary.

It still amazes me how everything started. If someone had told me back then that the girl who signed my clearance would become one of the most important people in my life, I probably would not have believed it.

I still remember November 7, 2025. It was clearance day, and the moment I saw you, I was captivated by your beauty. There was just something about you that caught my attention instantly. I could not stop thinking about you afterward. I was so determined to get to know you that I even begged my friend to get your Facebook account so I could send you a friend request.

When you accepted my request the next day, I was happier than I would ever admit. From that point on, I started doing everything I could to get your attention. I posted stories about my day, my studies, and even my study sessions, hoping you would notice me. Thankfully, it worked.

When you replied to one of my notes, that simple message became the beginning of countless conversations that I now cherish so much.

But our story was not easy from the start. While we were getting to know each other, your ex was trying to come back into your life. I remember how worried I was during that time. I was afraid of losing you before I even had the chance to truly be part of your life. There were moments when I felt lost because I did not know what would happen.

But when everything settled, you chose me.

I do not think I will ever fully be able to explain how grateful I am for that.

These past two months have shown me that you are more than just the girl I admired from afar. You have become my comfort, my support system, and one of the biggest reasons I keep pushing forward every day.

I am thankful for how we support each other in our studies. I am proud that we became Dean's Listers together, and I am even more proud of the effort and dedication you put into everything you do. Seeing you succeed inspires me to become better as well.

What means the most to me is that you stayed during the moments when life was not easy. There were days when I felt like I had nothing, even days when my wallet was empty and I was struggling. Yet you never made me feel alone. You stayed by my side, encouraged me, and helped me get through those difficult times.

That is something I will never forget.

As we celebrate our second monthsary, I want you to know that this is not the end of our story. It is only the beginning. We still have so many memories to create, so many dreams to chase, and so many challenges to overcome together.

Thank you for choosing me.

Thank you for staying.

Thank you for loving me.

No matter what happens, I will always appreciate the day our paths crossed and the chance I was given to love someone as wonderful as you.

Happy 2nd Monthsary, Girl.
